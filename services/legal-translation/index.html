Court-Ready Legal Translation in Dubai

Our legal translation services are certified by the UAE Ministry of Justice and accepted by all courts and government entities in the UAE. We translate legal documents in 43+ languages including Arabic, English, Russian, French, Chinese, Hindi, Urdu, Spanish, German, and more.

From contracts and pleadings to judgments and witness statements, we deliver accurate, formatted translations that meet the exacting standards of Dubai Courts, DIFC, and ADGM. Native-speaking translators for every language pair.
