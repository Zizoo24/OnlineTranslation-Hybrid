Frequently Asked Questions

How long does translation take?

Standard documents (birth certificates, degrees) are often completed within 2-4 hours. Complex legal contracts depend on word count and may require 24-48 hours. We provide exact timeframes in our quotation.

Is your translation accepted by courts?

Yes. Our strategic partner Arkan Legal Translation is fully certified by the UAE Ministry of Justice. Our translations are accepted by Dubai Courts, DIFC, ADGM, Notary Public, and all Government Ministries.

How do I send my documents?

Simply WhatsApp your documents to +971 50 862 0217. You can send PDFs, photos, or scans. Any format works. We review and send you a quotation within minutes.

Do you offer urgent same-day service?

Yes. Our express service provides urgent translation for critical court and immigration deadlines. Contact us to discuss your specific timeline and requirements.

We handle Arabic English legal translation in-house. For other language pairs, we coordinate with certified translators in 43+ languages to meet your requirements.
