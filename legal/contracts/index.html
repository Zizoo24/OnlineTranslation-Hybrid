Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use a photo from my phone?

Yes, you can. As long as all four corners of the document are clearly visible and the text is legible, a high-quality smartphone photo is acceptable. Please avoid shadows and ensure the document is flat.

Is this translation accepted for a Golden Visa application?

Yes, all our legal translations are certified by the UAE Ministry of Justice and are fully compliant with the requirements for Golden Visa applications, as well as other official submissions to bodies like GDRFA and ICA.
